About Us

The Kambal Lanyards, founded in August 2023, is a small business that sells university lanyards at Mapua University. Founded by Alexa Jena Ruanes and Andrea Paulene Real, who have been friends since high school, it offers stylish and high-quality 1-inch and 0.5-inch lanyards. Our mission is to provide Mapua students with trendy and functional accessories that reflect their university pride. With the help of YouTube and books, we learned and created our own business, "The Kambal."
